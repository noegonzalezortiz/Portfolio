I live in the small town of Stephentown, New York. People consider me an avid learner. My different studies have taken me to work as a teacher, office manager, medical interpreter, computer tech, handyman and woodworker.

One of my outstanding traits is that I am a hard working professional eager to help people with their tech/ web development needs.

About Me

I am a web development student, passionate about the tech world and the rapid growth of web development. Throughout this past year I have notice the need of more web development solutions to our everyday life situation. As a former computer lab teacher I beleive that there is no I in team, working together we can acomplish so much more.

Coming back to my tech roots have made me eager to learn and share my knowledge and experiences with others.
